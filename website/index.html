Conclusion

The visualization allows up to observe high level patterns such as common violations, or distribution of fines per country. Given the plots above, we conclude that the articles 5 and 6 may be similar in purpose since they are very often used together. Upon closer inspection they are indeed related as both deal with the rules and conditions of processing personal data. Furthermore, we find that the number of repeated offenders is relatively high. It goes up to 100% for companies in Portugal, indicating that the fines are not efficient incentive to prevent future violations.
